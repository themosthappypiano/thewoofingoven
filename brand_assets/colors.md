## Typography Hierarchy

### Primary Typeface: Avenir
- **Usage**: Body text, general content, paragraphs
- **Weights**: Light, Book, Medium, Bold, Extra Bold, Black
- **Italics**: Available for all weights

### Display Font: FTF Brotein  
- **Usage**: Headings, titles (small snippets only)
- **Character**: Playful, links brand with fun energy of dogs

### Script Font: Hittedal Script
- **Usage**: Descriptors, taglines like "Bake, Bark, Love!"
- **Character**: Warm, friendly, handmade touch

### Caps Font: Eveleth Dot
- **Usage**: Flavor descriptors, paragraph headings (all caps)
- **Character**: Bold, strong, rustic, natural
- **Weights**: Light, Regular, Bold
